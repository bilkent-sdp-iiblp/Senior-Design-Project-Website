Ubien includes guided AI support as a feature area for helping learners work through IBL questions. The intended behavior is different from a generic answer bot: the assistant should support inquiry by giving hints, asking follow-up questions, and clarifying concepts.

## Learning Goal

In IBL, the process of reasoning through a question is as important as the final answer. AI support should therefore encourage progress without short-circuiting the learning process.

## Interaction Pattern

- The learner opens a question inside a course activity
- The platform provides the assistant with relevant course context
- The assistant responds with guidance aligned with the current question
- The learner continues working through the activity instead of receiving only a final answer

## Accuracy and Oversight

AI-generated responses can be useful, but they should not be treated as automatically authoritative. Ubien treats AI support as an assistant layer that stays grounded in the course material.
