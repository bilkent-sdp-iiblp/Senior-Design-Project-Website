The PDF course builder is one of the central feature areas of Ubien. It reduces the manual work required to move Inquiry-Based Learning (IBL) materials from a static document into a structured online course.

## Why This Matters

Many IBL resources are distributed as PDFs with chapters, sections, problems, and guiding questions. Without tooling, users may need to manually copy content into a learning platform and rebuild the original structure by hand.

Ubien treats the PDF as a source document for a course.

## Workflow

- Read available PDF structure, such as bookmarks and headings
- Identify course sections, activities, and question blocks
- Convert the extracted structure into course modules
- Keep the output editable so users can correct or refine it

The goal is not to remove human judgment. The goal is to make structured course creation faster and easier to revise.

## Reliability Priority

Reliability is important for this feature. When a source PDF has missing or inconsistent structure, the system should make that limitation clear instead of silently producing a misleading course.
