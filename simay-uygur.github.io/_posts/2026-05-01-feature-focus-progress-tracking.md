Progress tracking is a practical feature area for making longer IBL courses easier to use. Learners should be able to see what they have completed, where they stopped, and which questions still need attention.

## What Ubien Tracks

Ubien tracks progress at multiple levels:

- Course completion
- Section or module completion
- Slide or step completion
- Individual question status

This structure gives the platform enough detail to support learner-facing progress views and course summaries.

## Why Granular Progress Helps

IBL courses often require sustained work across a sequence of related questions. Granular progress tracking helps learners resume work without losing context and gives the team a foundation for clearer course analytics later in the project.

## Design Constraint

Progress indicators should be informative without becoming distracting. The learner should always understand what to do next, while the interface stays focused on the course activity itself.
