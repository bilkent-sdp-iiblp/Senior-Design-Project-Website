Ubien includes animated avatars that make course content more engaging during slides and questions. The system supports multiple avatar styles, including male and female avatars, so course creators can choose a presentation style that fits their material.

## Natural Talking with Slide Context

Avatar speech is generated from the slide content, so explanations stay aligned with what learners are currently viewing. This helps the avatar speak in a more natural teaching flow instead of disconnected short prompts.

## Text-to-Speech and Talking Style

Ubien uses Amazon Polly for text-to-speech. This gives avatars clear spoken delivery while keeping narration synchronized with the course content.

## Body Animation and Expression

Avatars can stay idle when appropriate or switch to expressive talking behavior while explaining a concept. They also show emotions during instruction to make lessons feel more dynamic and engaging.

## Why This Helps

Voice, motion, and emotion together make online inquiry sessions feel more interactive, helping learners stay focused as they move through slides and questions.
